Need Of Sensors

Soil NPK sensors are used to detect the content of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in the soil and judge the fertility of the soil by detecting the conductivity transformation caused by different concentrations in the soil.03 They are low-cost, quick-responding, reasonably accurate, and portable sensors that can be interconnected with Arduino for real-time monitoring of NPK nutrient content in soil for smart agriculture.4 The JXCT Soil NPK sensor is a low-cost, quick-responding, high-precision, and portable sensor that works with Modbus RS485.2 It is suitable for detecting the content of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in the soil and is also known as the soil nutrient speed tester.13 The sensors are required for remote monitoring of soil health.5
